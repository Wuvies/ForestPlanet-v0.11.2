VII Apparel

VII Apparel is an athletic wear company based in Richmond, VA, and many of their items are made from recycled plastic bottles. The custom-designed fabric is lightweight, sweat-wicking, and antimicrobial.  VII Apparel plants one tree for each fully sublimated jersey sold.
